Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2, 2026

EventSwipe ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application EventSwipe: Events Near You (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account or use our App, you may provide us with:

  • Email address and password for account creation
  • Profile information (optional display name)
  • Event information if you post events (event details, images, descriptions)
  • Your interactions with events (saves, swipes)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your device's location to show you nearby events. You can disable location services at any time through your device settings.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App, including events viewed, saved, and time spent

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We receive event data from third-party services including Ticketmaster and SeatGeek to populate our event listings. This data includes publicly available event information such as event names, dates, venues, and ticket links.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Show you events near your location
  • Save your event preferences and saved events
  • Allow you to post and manage your own events
  • Improve and optimize the App experience
  • Communicate with you about updates or issues
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and storage services. Their privacy practices are governed by their respective privacy policies.
  • Event Organizers: If you interact with a user-posted event (such as clicking to get tickets), the event organizer may see aggregated, anonymized analytics.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Firebase services provided by Google. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections and secure authentication. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Location: Disable location services through your device settings
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from promotional communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

7. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as ticket purchasing sites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at:

privacy@eventswipe.app
